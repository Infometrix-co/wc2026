Section 1 — The Framework

Why archetypes beat budgets

Squad market value tells you who should win on paper. It doesn't tell you how a team actually plays, or what happens when two playing identities collide. A modest Counter Kings side drawn against a high-line Maestros giant is worth far more than its ranking suggests — the archetype clash is the signal the market isn't pricing.

The Moneyball anchor

Moneyball's lesson was never that statistics win games. It was that markets misprice what they don't measure. We classified all 48 teams using FBref qualifying stats, The Athletic tactical analysis, and squad data. Four archetypes that explain stylistic fit better than FIFA rankings or transfer budgets.

Survivorship bias, inverted

Most previews price teams on how strong they looked getting here. Tournament history keeps rewarding something else — clear identity, organisation, and a transition threat outlast more talented squads. The framework is built to spot that profile before the market does.
